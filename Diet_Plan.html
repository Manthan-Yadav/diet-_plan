Diet Plan for Muscle Gain & Fat Loss (79kg Male)

Daily Targets:

  • Calories: ~2800 kcal
  • Protein: ~160g
  • Carbohydrates: ~365g
  • Fats: ~80g

Important Note: The measurements for items like dal, rice, oats, and soya chunks are for their raw, uncooked weight. This is the most accurate way to track nutrition.

How to Use This Plan:

  • Invest in a Kitchen Scale: A small digital kitchen scale is the best tool to ensure accuracy.
  • Raw vs. Cooked: Remember to weigh items like lentils, legumes, rice, and oats before cooking.
  • Flexibility is Key: You can swap vegetables and types of dal/legumes. Keep the raw gram amount similar to maintain the nutritional value.
  • Listen to Your Body: This is a strong starting point. Assess your progress after 2-3 weeks and adjust portion sizes if needed.
